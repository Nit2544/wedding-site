Travel
⯅
Our wedding will take place in La Turballe, a small seaside town in north-western France. Getting there takes a little planning, especially for those travelling from abroad, but we hope the information below will help make your journey as smooth as possible.
Flying from Finland Helsinki -> Paris (CDG airport) direct flight - approx. 2h30 – 3h Helsinki -> Nantes – one layover – approx. 5h-7h
If you are flying to Paris, you can either rent a car or take a high-speed train to Nantes or Saint-Nazaire.
Taking the train from Paris Most westbound trains leave from Gare Montparnasse (70 minutes from CDG airport with public transport). We recommend allowing at least 2 hours between flight landing and train departure. The train journey from Paris to Nantes takes approximately 2 hours.
From Nantes When you reach Nantes, you can rent a car or continue your journey by train to Saint-Nazaire. There are direct trains to Saint-Nazaire from Paris, but you can also change to a regional train at Nantes railway station. Trains to Saint-Nazaire leave every hour and the journey takes about 45 minutes. For train schedules and bookings, please visit sncf.com.
Getting around locally (Saint-Nazaire – Guérande – La Turballe) If you choose not to rent a car, you can use the local bus network to get around the area. You can find out about the routes and schedule on https://www.lilapresquile.fr/ We do not recommend taxis, as availability is limited and prices can be quite high. On the wedding day, we will organize a shuttle service to and from the wedding venue, departing from Guérande and possibly Saint-Nazaire (more details to follow).
Our recommendation Fly to Paris (CDG) → train to Nantes → rent a car in Nantes → enjoy your stay! If you have any questions while planning your trip, don’t hesitate to contact us — we’ll be very happy to help.
