How to get there?
Our wedding will take place in La Turballe, a charming seaside town on the Atlantic coast of France, about 1 hour west of Nantes. Below you’ll find helpful information to plan your trip, especially if you’re traveling from Finland.
From Finland to France ᯓ✈︎
Most guests coming from Finland will find it easiest to fly into one of the following airports:
Nantes Atlantique Airport (NTE) – Closest major airport, approx. 1h30 from La Turballe. Direct flights may not always be available, but convenient connections often go through Paris, Amsterdam, Copenhagen, or Frankfurt.
Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) or Paris Orly (ORY) From Paris, you can either take a domestic flight to Nantes or a high-speed train to Nantes (2h).

From Paris to Nantes
If you land in Paris and prefer the train:
Take the TGV (high-speed train) from Paris Montparnasse to Nantes – Journey time: about 2 hours
From the Nantes train station, you can rent a car or take a regional train towards the coast.
For purchasing train tickets: www.sncf-connect.com
