Presqu’île de Guérande

Right around La Turballe, the Presqu’île de Guérande offers a wonderful mix of coastline, heritage, and local traditions. It is a perfect area for relaxed walks, enjoying the ocean, and discovering the character of the region.

You can enjoy:
✦ Beaches and coastal paths along the Atlantic
✦ Charming and pitoresque seaside towns
✦ The breathtaking salt marshes
✦ The medieval walled town of Guérande
          
More on the tourist office of La Baule - Guérande website:
          
https://en.labaule-guerande.com/
          
Saint-Nazaire

About half an hour away, Saint-Nazaire offers a different atmosphere, closely linked to the sea and its industrial and maritime past. The city is known for its major shipyards and its role in transatlantic ocean liner history. It combines cultural visits with coastal scenery, making it a great option for a half-day or day trip.

          <span class="style-se-h2 centered">Nantes
          <span class="style-se-p1 justified">
Located just over an hour from La Turballe, Nantes is a vibrant and creative city, perfect for a full day of exploration. Its historic center, cultural life, and lively neighborhoods offer a nice contrast with the coast. Visitors can combine history, museums, shopping, and restaurants in one visit.
          
          
          <span class="style-se-p1 justified">
Among the city’s best-known attractions are the Château des Ducs de Bretagne and Les Machines de l’Île, along with many other cultural and architectural discoveries throughout the city.
